GOV DATASET SUMMARY

Dataset used:
gov_cleaned_standardised.csv

Main GOV analysis completed:
1. Monthly job counts
2. Category counts
3. Monthly counts for top 5 categories

Key findings:
- The dataset covers 5 months only: 2023-07 to 2023-11
- The monthly distribution is very uneven
- Most records are concentrated in October 2023 and November 2023
- Healthcare & Nursing Jobs is the largest category
- Other major categories include Other/General Jobs, Teaching Jobs, Logistics & Warehouse Jobs, and Social Work Jobs

Limitations:
- The time range is too short for strong forecasting
- Early months appear incomplete or much smaller than later months
- The dataset is more useful for exploratory descriptive analysis than robust forecasting

Project use:
- GOV will be used as supporting labour-market context
- GOV will not be treated as the main skill extraction dataset
- GOV analysis is now paused so the project can move back to the skill-focused pipeline
